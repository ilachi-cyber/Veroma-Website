Who we are
What is Veroma Media?

Veroma Media is a senior-led PR, branding and digital marketing agency headquartered in Mumbai, with offices in Delhi and Dubai. The studio helps startups, market leaders and founder-led brands grow by combining brand strategy, public relations, social media, performance marketing, content and distribution into a single integrated growth system, measured on outcomes, not outputs.

Every ambitious brand starts with a spark, a big idea, a bold move, or the conviction that things can be done better. Veroma Media exists to turn that spark into momentum. What began as a handful of marketers and strategists is now a tight, interdisciplinary team of creators, analysts and problem-solvers who help brands across India and beyond not just compete, but lead, connect and be remembered.

We believe in sharp strategy, genuine partnerships and stories that matter. Our mission is simple: make marketing personal, measurable and exciting again, for startups, for market leaders, and for everyone ready to break the mould. As a branding and digital marketing partner, we don't hand clients a deck and walk away; we sit inside the growth problem with them until the numbers move.

With every engagement we stay true to what sets us apart: smart, outcome-driven solutions, relentless curiosity, and the conviction that results and relationships matter most. It's why most of our clients stay for years, not months, and why our work spans premium D2C, financial services, real estate, media and entertainment, and global trade.

Leadership
CEO & Managing Director

Ilachi Nagada

A commercial strategist and partnerships lead, Ilachi focuses on alliances, scaling and revenue operations, helping clients unlock efficient growth across channels and markets. He founded Veroma Media on a simple belief: that brand, PR and performance should never be separate departments fighting for credit, but one compounding system pointed at a single outcome.

Under his leadership, Veroma has grown from a boutique studio into an integrated growth partner trusted by founders building category-defining companies, from premium consumer brands to financial and media ventures across Mumbai, Delhi and Dubai.

Our approach

Strategy. Creativity. Relentless execution.

At Veroma, results come from the disciplined combination of strategy, creativity and relentless execution. Every engagement begins with discovery, your market, your audience, your economics and your goals, before we craft the right mix of branding, PR and digital marketing to drive measurable growth.

We combine industry research, data-driven decision-making and bold creativity to help brands stand out, build trust and turn interest into action. Strategy informs story. Story fuels distribution. Distribution generates data. Data sharpens strategy. The loop compounds month after month, and the longer you run it, the cheaper your next outcome becomes. Every partnership is built on transparent communication and a relentless focus on ROI.
